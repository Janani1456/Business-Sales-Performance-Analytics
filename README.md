Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Task 1. The objective was to analyze business sales data and create a professional dashboard that helps answer key business questions related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic sales distribution.

The analysis was performed using Power BI on the Online Retail dataset.

Business Problem

Businesses need data-driven insights to understand:

Which products generate the highest revenue?
How does revenue change over time?
Which countries contribute the most sales?
Who are the most valuable customers?
Where should the business focus to increase growth?

This dashboard provides answers to these questions through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

Dataset

Dataset: Online Retail Dataset

The dataset contains:

Invoice Information
Product Details
Quantity Sold
Unit Price
Customer Information
Country Information
Transaction Dates

A Revenue column was created using:

Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price


Tools Used
Power BI
Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Business Intelligence & Visualization

Dashboard Features
1. Monthly Revenue Trend

Analyzes revenue performance across different months and identifies seasonal sales patterns.

2. Top Countries by Revenue

Identifies countries generating the highest revenue outside the United Kingdom.

3. Top Products by Revenue

Highlights products contributing the most to overall revenue.

4. Top Customers by Revenue

Identifies high-value customers responsible for a significant share of sales.

5. Global Product Demand Map

Visualizes product demand across countries using geographic analysis.

6. Interactive Country Filter

Allows users to explore sales performance for individual countries.

Key Insights
Revenue reached its highest level during November, indicating strong seasonal demand.
The Netherlands generated the highest revenue among countries outside the United Kingdom.
A small number of customers contribute a large percentage of total revenue.
Product sales are concentrated among a few high-performing products.
International demand is strongest across European countries.

Since the Online Retail dataset did not contain product categories, country-level revenue analysis was performed to identify high-value regions.

Business Recommendations
Increase inventory before peak demand periods, especially during Q4.
Focus marketing efforts on countries generating high revenue.
Implement customer retention and loyalty programs for top customers.
Promote top-performing products through targeted campaigns.
Explore expansion opportunities in high-performing international markets.

This dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify growth opportunities, understand customer behavior, and make data-driven decisions.
